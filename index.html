About Us

We are a small and vibrant family-owned café in the centre of Mentone right across the road to the train station. We strive everyday on producing the best quality, freshly brewed coffee as well as a variety of home-made and tasty food. Our goal is to become the go-to café for people working, visiting and living in the area. If you are around the Mentone vicinity, do come in and say hi to our friendly staff.
